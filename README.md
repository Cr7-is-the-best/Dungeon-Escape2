What this game is about -This game is about placing units/towers and not let the enemy throuth like a tower defense game as the game goes on the enemy units get stronger
Why I made this game -I made this game becouse I already have a Dungeon Escape Game so I said i should make more parts to it instead of going 3d wich is hard in python i went for a simplir opton a tower defense game.

You get to pick between 3 diffrent maps & There is also a leaderboard

You start on wave 1 and try to get to 100 there is a 3 secound countdown after each wave is complete
You start with 120gold
You also start with 100 lives

You get 5 towers wich each do a differnt amount of damage

Controles
Key / Action	What it does
Left click Place selected tower
Left click on that tower to Upgrade it (up to LV 3)
Right click on the tower to Sell it for 50% 
1 – 5	Select tower type
Enter = start

Upgrades
★ = LV 1 — base stats
★★	= LV 2 — +50% damage, +20% range but costs 3.5 times more then level 1
★★★ = LV 3 — +120% damage, +40% range but costs 7 times more then level 1

Your Units
Your First Unit is the rifle he costs 50 gold and does 40 damage
Your Secound Unit is the shotgun he costs 150 gold and does 25 damage
Your Third Unit is the sniper he costs 300 gold and does 130 damage
Yor Fourth Unit is the minigun he costs 450 gold and does 25 damage per bullet
Your Last Unit is the rpg he costs 600 gold and does 80 damage

Enemy Units
The First enemy unit is normal if he gets to the end you lose 10 lives, but if you destroy that unit you get 30 gold,
The Secound enemy unit is fast he goes faster then all the units if he gets to the end you lose 5 lives, if you destroy that unit you get 50 gold,
The Last enemy unit is tank it is stronger then the other enemy units and if he gets to the end you lose 20 lives, if you destroy that unit you get 75 gold.
